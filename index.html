OUR STORY

About Humpty's Dumplings.

After a night out at the bar, our owners put together a late-night snack of rotisserie chicken fried in wont ton wrappers -- and it was a hit! That was awhile ago, and after a lot more experimenting and some hard work, we're proud to say we've been in business serving our unique dumpling dishes for about four years now. From Buffalo chicken to cheese steak, our dumplings come in flavors that are anything but run-of-the-mill, and we've become pretty popular here in Glenside. We're looking forward to expanding in the future and sharing our food with even more people.
